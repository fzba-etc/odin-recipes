Basic Vanilla Cupcakes

Image sourced from here.

Fluffy golden cupcakes with swirls of thick, creamy frosting! A delicious recipe that you will have over and over again, whether you customize or just want to have it plain.

Ingredients

Cake

  • 1/2 cup (or 1 stick) butter, room temperature
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs, room temperature
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 3/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk

Frosting

  • 1 (8 oz) package cream cheese, room temperature
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups cold heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

Cake

  1. Cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  2. Add in eggs, one at a time, beating after each addition.
  3. Mix in vanilla extract.
  4. Whisk together flour and baking powder.
  5. Add dry ingredient to wet ingredients and mix. Batter will be thick.
  6. Mix in milk.
  7. Scoop a heaping tablespoon of batter into the bottom of each cupcake liner.
  8. Bake for 20-25 minutes at 350F.

Frosting

  1. Whip heavy cream to stiff peaks (1-2 minutes).
  2. Cream together cream cheese and powdered sugar.
  3. Add in vanilla.
  4. Fold in whipped cream.
