Feta & Mozzarella Pastries paired with Red Lentil Soup (Shorbet Addas)

An aromatic, warm lentil soup with melt-in-your-mouth creamy feta pastries served on the side.

Ingredients

Soup

  • 2 cups red lentils, washed
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp white pepper
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 6 cups boiling water
  • 1 chicken bullion cube

Pastries

  • Puff pastry (1 box, use both sheets)
  • 3 cups mozzarella
  • 1 1/4 cup feta
  • Handful freshly chopped parsley

Steps

Soup

  1. Wash your lentils very well.
  2. In a pot, on medium heat sauté your onion in olive oil. Once translucent and aromatic, throw in your spices & your washed lentils.
  3. Pour in 6 cups of boiling water & 1 chicken bullion cube. Let it reach a full boil all together, then cover & let simmer on medium heat for 30 minutes(stirring frequently).
  4. After 30 min, serve with lemon, green onions, radishes, and olives if you’d like.

Pastries

  1. Roll put your puff pastry sheets & cut into squares.
  2. Add your filling & fold into triangles. Make sure to pinch them closed tightly bc we don’t want the cheese to ooze out when we bake them (flour your hands if you find the dough to be difficult to work with).
  3. Add to a prepared baking sheet, brush your pastries with egg wash and bake at 400°f for around 15 minutes. Every oven performs definitely, so make sure to keep your eye on them.
  4. They should be golden on the top & bottom.
